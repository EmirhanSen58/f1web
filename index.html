Latest News

Max Verstappen is FOUR Times World Champion.

Max Verstappen officially celebrated his fourth Formula 1 world championship title at the 2024 FIA Awards Ceremony. The FIA's celebration of its world champions took place this year in Rwanda's capital Kigali.

Mclaren became constructors champion after 26 years.

Mclaren beat Ferraris in the race for the constructors championship. Lando Norris and Oscar piastri won the team championship, while Lando Norris lost the drivers' championship to Max Verstappen.

Last race is Abu Dhabi.

The constructors championship is down to the last race. Ferrari and Mclaren have a chance for the championship, while RedBull Racing finished 3rd with Perez's poor performance.
